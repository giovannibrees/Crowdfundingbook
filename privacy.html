Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 July 2026

This is the privacy policy for giovannibreesbook.com, the official website of Giovanni Brees, author of The Crowdfunding Quick-Guide. The short version: this site is a simple, static book page. It sets no cookies, runs no analytics, and uses no tracking or advertising technology.

Information we collect

We do not collect, store or process any personal information through this website. There are no accounts, no sign-up forms, no newsletters and no comment sections.

Hosting and server logs

The site is served by our hosting and content-delivery providers (Cloudflare and GitHub Pages). Like virtually all web hosts, they may keep short-lived technical logs (such as IP address, browser type and pages requested) to operate and secure their networks. Those logs are governed by the providers' own privacy policies; we do not use them to identify visitors.

Fonts

Web fonts are loaded from Google Fonts. When your browser fetches a font, your IP address is technically shared with Google as part of that request. Google states that font requests are not used for profiling and are not correlated with other Google services.

Links to other websites

This site links to third-party websites, including Amazon (to purchase the book) and LinkedIn. Once you leave this site, those services' own privacy policies apply. We receive no data about what you do there.

Email

If you choose to email us, we receive the information you send (your address and your message) and use it only to reply. We do not add you to any mailing list or share your details with anyone.

Your rights

Since we hold no personal data about visitors, there is normally nothing for us to access, correct or delete. If you have emailed us and would like that correspondence removed, just ask and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

If the site ever changes in a way that affects your privacy (for example, adding a newsletter), this page will be updated first and the date above revised.
